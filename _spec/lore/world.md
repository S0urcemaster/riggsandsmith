# World Scenario

## Definition

The world is futuristic, but not radically alien.

It is still recognizably human.

People are broadly the same as they are today.

Their desires, fears, ambitions, weaknesses, and social behavior remain familiar.

## Social Order

The overall social structure is also broadly familiar.

There are still:

- wealthy elites
- corporate power
- state power
- criminal networks
- precarious ordinary lives

Technology has advanced, but power remains unevenly distributed.

New tools have not made society fair.

They have mostly made control, profit, surveillance, and manipulation more powerful.

## Technology Level

The main difference from today is the strength and reach of technology.

Systems are denser, more connected, more adaptive, and more invasive.

Advanced devices can influence finance, surveillance, probability, and machine behavior in ways that would seem impossible today.

This creates a world where rare illegal technologies can open cracks inside otherwise highly controlled systems.

## Tone Rule

The setting should not feel like a distant space civilization.

It should feel like an intensified future version of today's world:

- same human nature
- similar power relations
- stronger systems
- deeper dependence on technology
- higher stakes when someone finds a way to bend the rules
